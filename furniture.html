Geode

From Stardew Valley Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

The Geode is a Mineral deposit that contains minerals, artifacts, ores, or basic resources. It can be obtained from breaking rocks above ground in Stardew Valley and in The Mines (especially floors 1-39), or dropped by Duggies. It may also be found in Fishing Treasure Chests or received as a gift at the Feast of the Winter Star. A Stonefish Fish Pond may produce 1, 5, or 30 Geodes when its population reaches 9.

The geode can be broken open by Clint in the Blacksmith shop for 25g.

Gifting

Tailoring

Geode is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Gray Hoodie.

Quests

  • 2 Geodes may be requested by Sunfish in a Fish Pond quest to increase the capacity of the pond from 3 to 5.
  • 5 Geodes may be requested by Stonefish in a Fish Pond quest to increase the capacity of the pond from 5 to 7.

External Links
